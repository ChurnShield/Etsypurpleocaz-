## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Waiting for Perfect
Don't spend weeks perfecting your launch. Ship now, iterate later.

### ❌ Ignoring Outreach Volume
10 emails won't cut it. You need 100+ emails to get 10 replies to get 2 customers.

### ❌ Not Following Up
80% of sales happen after 5+ follow-ups. Most people give up after 1.

### ❌ Generic Messages
"Hi, check out my product" gets 0% replies. Personalize the first line.

### ❌ Not Tracking Metrics
If you're not tracking, you're not improving. Update your spreadsheet daily.
