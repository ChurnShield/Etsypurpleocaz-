## Post Content:

```
Hey Indie Hackers! 👋

I just built **ChurnShield** - a tool that intercepts subscription cancellations and shows personalized retention offers (discounts, pauses, feature highlights) to save churning customers.

**The problem:** Most SaaS companies lose 5-7% MRR to churn monthly. When customers click "cancel," they're just... gone.

**The solution:** ChurnShield asks why they're leaving and shows the right offer based on their reason. Average save rate: 20-40%.

## 🎯 Looking for 5 beta testers

**What I'm offering:**
- ✅ Free lifetime access (normally $99-299/mo)
- ✅ Direct support from me (founder)
- ✅ Shape the product with your feedback
- ✅ Get featured in our launch case studies

**What I need from you:**
- SaaS business with $1K+ MRR (so there's actual churn to test)
- Using Stripe for payments
- Willing to test for 2-3 weeks
- Share honest feedback (good and bad)

## 🔧 What you'll do:

1. Connect your Stripe account (OAuth, takes 30 seconds)
2. Build a cancellation flow (5-10 minutes, no code)
3. Let it run for 2-3 weeks
4. Tell me what works and what doesn't

## 💰 What's in it for you:

If you're losing even $500/month to churn, and ChurnShield saves 30%, that's **$150/month recovered = $1,800/year**.

Plus you get to:
- Keep lifetime free access
- Influence the product roadmap
- Get early access to AI-powered offer optimization (coming soon)
```
