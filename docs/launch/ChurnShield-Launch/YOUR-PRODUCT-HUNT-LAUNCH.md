## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Launching on Friday/Weekend
Product Hunt traffic is lowest on weekends. Launch Tuesday-Thursday.

### ❌ Not Posting First Comment Quickly
If you don't post your maker story within 5-10 minutes, someone else will comment first and steal the top spot.

### ❌ Being Too Salesy
Product Hunt users hate hard selling. Be authentic, share your story, ask for feedback.

### ❌ Not Responding to Comments
Engagement drives ranking. Respond to EVERY comment, even negative ones (politely).

### ❌ Terrible Screenshots
Blurry, cluttered, or confusing screenshots kill conversions. Spend time making them crystal clear.

### ❌ Launching Without Traffic Plan
Don't rely on Product Hunt alone. Have social, email, communities ready to drive traffic.

**Q: When is the best time to launch?**
