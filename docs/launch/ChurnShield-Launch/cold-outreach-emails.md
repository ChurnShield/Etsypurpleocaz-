# Cold Outreach Email Templates - ChurnShield

## 🎯 Target Audience

**Ideal Customer Profile:**
- SaaS businesses with $5K-$100K MRR
- B2B or B2C subscription models
- Using Stripe for payments
- Experiencing 5%+ monthly churn
- 3-50 person teams

**Where to Find Them:**
1. Indie Hackers (SaaS founders)
2. MicroConf community
3. SaaS subreddits

## Email Sequence Strategy

**Sequence Structure:**
- Email 1: Problem-focused (send Monday/Tuesday)
- Email 2: Case study follow-up (wait 3 days)
- Email 3: Specific value prop (wait 3 days)
- Email 4: Last chance + urgency (wait 3 days)

**Best Practices:**
- Send between 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
- Personalize first line (research their product)
- Keep under 100 words
- One clear CTA
- Subject line under 50 characters

**Subject:** "Quick question about [Company] churn"
**OR:** "[Name] - losing customers?"


- Provides context (industry benchmarks)
- Soft CTA (curious if relevant)

## Metrics to Track

### Email Performance
- **Open rate:** 40%+ (good), 30-40% (average), <30% (fix subject lines)
- **Reply rate:** 10%+ (good), 5-10% (average), <5% (fix copy)
- **Demo booking rate:** 30%+ of replies (good), 20-30% (average)
- **Close rate:** 20%+ of demos (good), 10-20% (average)

### Volume Targets
- Week 1: 20 emails → 2 demos → 1 customer
- Week 2: 100 emails → 10 demos → 2 customers
- Week 3: 200 emails → 15 demos → 3 customers
- Week 4: 300 emails → 20 demos → 4 customers

**Total Month 1: 10+ customers = $500-$1,000 MRR**
