**Full Version (Product Hunt description):**
```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Andy, and I built ChurnShield after watching SaaS companies lose thousands to preventable churn.

## The Problem
When customers click "cancel subscription," most businesses just... let them go. 🤯

But here's the thing: 40% of churning customers would stay if given the right offer at the right time.

## The Solution
ChurnShield is a cancellation interception platform that:

✅ **Smart Cancel Flows** - Build custom flows with offers, surveys, and pauses
✅ **Churn Risk Analysis** - Identify at-risk customers before they cancel
✅ **Recovery Inbox** - Manage cancellation cases with team collaboration
✅ **Stripe Integration** - Set up in 5 minutes with OAuth
✅ **Real-Time Analytics** - Track saves, revenue recovered, and offer performance

## How It Works
1. Customer clicks "Cancel Subscription"
2. They're redirected to your branded ChurnShield flow
3. ChurnShield shows personalized offers (discount, pause, downgrade)
4. Customer accepts offer = subscription saved
5. You get notified + see analytics in real-time

## Who It's For
- SaaS founders tired of watching MRR walk away
- Customer success teams managing churn manually
- Subscription businesses with >$5K MRR

## Why I Built This
I was consulting for a SaaS company losing $30K/month to churn. We built a manual cancellation flow and saved 35% of cancellations = $10K recovered monthly.

I realized every SaaS needs this, but building it in-house takes months. So I built ChurnShield.

## What's Next
🔮 **Coming Soon:** AI-powered offer optimization (learns which offers work best for each customer segment)

## Special Offer for Product Hunt

Try it free for 14 days. No credit card required.

---

**I'd love your feedback!** What features would make this a must-have for your SaaS?
```
